Platforms

Platforms and vendors

Most municipal programs use Allen-Bradley ControlLogix / CompactLogix with Studio 5000, PanelView, and FactoryTalk. Host SCADA may be AVEVA Wonderware or GE iFIX per owner standard. Field instruments are specified per project—Siemens, Endress+Hauser, Rosemount, Hach, and others as required. We remain an independent integrator: platforms and devices are chosen for the application, not a manufacturer quota.

Process

Project phases

Delivery is structured around design submittals, shop fabrication, software development, witnessed testing, site work, and turnover—each phase supported by documentation suitable for consultant review and owner records.

01

Design

Architecture, schematics, panel layouts, PLC/SCADA structure—aligned to owner standards.

02

Fabrication

CSA shop build, labeling, internal test—photo-documented against approved drawings.

03

Software

Logic, HMI, SCADA graphics, historians, network integration—ready for FAT.

04

FAT

Witnessed I/O and sequence testing—protocols, punch lists, sign-off.

05

Commissioning

Loops, rotation, interlocks, alarms, tuning—with operations present.

06

Turnover

Training, native files, O&M—municipal closeout and warranty alignment.
