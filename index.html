What we deliver

Complete control systems from engineering through commissioning

CSA-certified panels, maintainable PLC logic, operator-grade SCADA graphics, and process instrumentation—integrated, tested, and documented for municipal and industrial automation programs.

Control panels

CSA-listed industrial control panels for indoor and outdoor service. PLC/SCADA enclosures, duplex/triplex pump panels, VFD assemblies, MCC sections, and network cabinets—NEMA 3R/4/4X rated with complete drawings, submittals, and certification.

PLC programming

Control logic for pumps, blowers, mixers, chemical systems, and utility processes. Clear, maintainable ladder logic with sequences, interlocks, and alarm structures—organized for future support staff and integrators.

SCADA / HMI systems

Operator interfaces for status, control, alarms, and trending. Touch panels and PC SCADA with plant-satellite linkage, remote visibility, and historian integration—built for shift turnover and upset conditions.

Instrumentation

Flow, level, pressure, and analytical instruments for water and wastewater processes. Magnetic flowmeters, ultrasonic level, hydrostatic transmitters, and water-quality sensors—configured, loop-checked, and commissioned.

VFD / starter integration

Variable frequency drives for pumps and blowers, soft starters, and full-voltage motor control. PLC/SCADA coordination for speed control, bypass sequencing, and protection—with PID tuning for level, pressure, and flow applications.

Industrial networking

Industrial Ethernet, EtherNet/IP, Modbus TCP/RTU, and secure remote access. Network-ready controls coordinated with IT and operations standards—from field devices through plant SCADA and historian systems.

FAT / SAT / commissioning

Factory acceptance testing with I/O verification, communication checks, and HMI dry testing. Site commissioning with loop checks, rotation verification, interlock testing, alarm validation, and PID tuning under real process conditions.

Documentation / training

Shop drawings, schematics, terminal schedules, and as-built packages. Native PLC/HMI files, tag databases, and operation narratives. On-site training for operations and maintenance staff—aligned with municipal closeout requirements.

Delivery process

Structured milestones from design through support

Phased delivery aligned with procurement, fabrication, testing, and commissioning milestones—documented for municipal closeout and audit requirements.

01

Design & engineering

Scope alignment, submittal preparation, and control architecture. Electrical schematics, panel layouts, PLC structure, and SCADA design—coordinated with owner standards and consultant specifications.

02

Panel fabrication

CSA-certified shop build with photo documentation. Component assembly, wiring, labeling, and internal testing—aligned with approved shop drawings and submittal schedules.

03

Programming & graphics

PLC logic development, HMI screen creation, and SCADA graphics. Alarm rationalization, historian configuration, and network integration—prepared for factory acceptance testing.

04

Factory acceptance test

Witnessed FAT with I/O verification, communication checks, and simulated process sequences. Documented test protocols, punch lists, and sign-off—before shipment to site.

05

Site commissioning

Installation support, loop checks, rotation verification, and interlock testing. PID tuning under real process conditions, alarm validation, and SAT coordination—with operations staff present.

06

Training & support

Operator training, maintenance orientation, and as-built documentation turnover. Native software files, tag databases, and O&M manuals—aligned with municipal closeout and warranty requirements.
