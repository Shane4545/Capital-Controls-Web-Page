Reference Overview

Scope

One scope. One voice. One system.

Components do not run plants—integrated systems do. We stay responsible from approved drawings through FAT, commissioning, and turnover, so operations inherit a coherent control story, not a pile of vendor handoffs.

Integrated delivery

Enclosures, PLC, SCADA, instruments, and networks engineered together against the same P&IDs and standards packages your consultants and owners expect.

Drawings, logic, graphics, and loop checks reference the same tags and narratives—auditable, maintainable, defensible.

Operational accountability

FAT proves the system before shipment. SAT proves it in process. Training and as-builts close the loop with the people who run the facility.

We do not disappear at mechanical completion—we work through sign-off.

Markets

Sectors we support

Primary experience in municipal water and wastewater, lift and booster pumping, and related infrastructure—plus landfills, boiler and thermal systems, hydro-related facilities, airports, and other industrial plants, mirroring the sectors long listed on capitalcontrols.ca. Clients include municipal, provincial, and federal programs as well as private owners.

Water treatment

Filtration, chemical feed, disinfection, booster systems—PLC modernization, SCADA upgrades, and instrumentation integration.

Wastewater

Screening through disinfection and biosolids—lagoon and secondary upgrades, alarm rationalization, operator training.

Pumping stations

Sanitary, storm, subdivision, and booster stations—VFD control, telemetry, unattended operation design.

Transit utilities

Station pumping and interlocks for TTC, Metrolinx, LRT, OC Transpo—coordination with authority standards.

Bridge & marine

Movable bridge control—HMI, interlocks, hydraulic and mechanical coordination for marine and roadway safety.

Industrial

Material handling, institutional utilities, selective process lines—same integration discipline outside municipal core.

Landfills & leachate

Leachate collection, pumping, and treatment controls—documented logic and SCADA suitable for environmental compliance and long-term O&M.

Boiler & thermal

Boiler and combustion-related control work where clients need defensible sequences, interlocks, and operator interfaces.

Hydro generation

Operational and balance-of-plant controls for small hydro and related infrastructure when scope aligns with our integration model.

Airports & major facilities

Utility pumping, standby systems, and building-related operational controls for airport and large-facility clients.

Platforms

Platforms and vendors

Most municipal programs use Allen-Bradley ControlLogix / CompactLogix with Studio 5000, PanelView, and FactoryTalk. Host SCADA may be AVEVA Wonderware or GE iFIX per owner standard. Field instruments are specified per project—Siemens, Endress+Hauser, Rosemount, Hach, and others as required. We remain an independent integrator: platforms and devices are chosen for the application, not a manufacturer quota.

AVEVA Wonderware

GE iFIX

Instrumentation

Process

Project phases

Delivery is structured around design submittals, shop fabrication, software development, witnessed testing, site work, and turnover—each phase supported by documentation suitable for consultant review and owner records.

01

Design

Architecture, schematics, panel layouts, PLC/SCADA structure—aligned to owner standards.

02

Fabrication

CSA shop build, labeling, internal test—photo-documented against approved drawings.

03

Software

Logic, HMI, SCADA graphics, historians, network integration—ready for FAT.

04

FAT

Witnessed I/O and sequence testing—protocols, punch lists, sign-off.

05

Commissioning

Loops, rotation, interlocks, alarms, tuning—with operations present.

06

Turnover

Training, native files, O&M—municipal closeout and warranty alignment.
