Reference Overview

Authority

One scope. One voice. One system.

Components do not run plants—integrated systems do. We stay responsible from approved drawings through FAT, commissioning, and turnover, so operations inherit a coherent control story, not a pile of vendor handoffs.

Integrated delivery

Enclosures, PLC, SCADA, instruments, and networks engineered together against the same P&IDs and standards packages your consultants and owners expect.

Drawings, logic, graphics, and loop checks reference the same tags and narratives—auditable, maintainable, defensible.

Operational accountability

FAT proves the system before shipment. SAT proves it in process. Training and as-builts close the loop with the people who run the facility.

We do not disappear at mechanical completion—we work through sign-off.

Control panels

PLC programming

SCADA / HMI

Platforms

Platforms and vendors

Most municipal programs use Allen-Bradley ControlLogix / CompactLogix with Studio 5000, PanelView, and FactoryTalk. Host SCADA may be AVEVA Wonderware or GE iFIX per owner standard.
