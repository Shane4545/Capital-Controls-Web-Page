From real CCI quotations

What “scope of supply” means on our quotes

The sentences below are taken from CCI-T-338Q rev D (Carleton Place WTP/WWTP, dated April 2025)—the same PDF package we issue to bidders. Your tender may differ, but this is how we define inclusions so procurement, the GC, and the owner see the same boundaries we defend at FAT and SAT.

Capital Controls & Instrumentation Inc. is pleased to offer the following quotation for your review:

Scope of supply

Supply of panels, starters (VFD, SS and FVNR) and instrumentation, as per attached lists. Any panel, starter or instrument not specifically listed on the lists below as “provided by Capital Controls” are not part of this scope.

Panels

Panel supply includes drawings, submittals, CSA certification and commissioning.

PLC / SCADA / HMI

PLC/SCADA/HMI panels include software and programming.

Starters

Starter panels include drawings, submittals, CSA certification, configuration and commissioning.

Instrumentation

Instrumentation includes drawings, submittals, configuration and commissioning.

Milestones (same quotation)

  • 5% on PO issuance
  • 25% on shop drawing sign-off
  • 60% on delivery of items
  • 10% on commissioning

Typical exclusions—called out on the same quote

Examples from the same document: vendor-supplied instrumentation and VFD packages, TSH sensors tied to pumps/motors, valves except block-and-bleed where specified with CCI-supplied items, permits and studies, mechanical/electrical installation unless listed, confined space unless listed, and liquidated-damages language we take exception to. Installation stays with others unless the line item says otherwise.
