Capability

What we deliver

One scope: fabrication, software, graphics, instruments, networks, testing, and turnover—structured for infrastructure programs that cannot afford ambiguity.

Control panels

CSA-listed builds—PLC/SCADA enclosures, pump panels, VFD assemblies, MCC sections—with drawings, submittals, and certification.

PLC programming

Maintainable ladder and structure for pumps, blowers, chemical systems, and utilities—interlocks and alarms organized for long-term support.

SCADA / HMI

Operator layers for status, control, alarms, and trending—touch panels and host SCADA with historian and remote visibility where required.

Instrumentation

Flow, level, pressure, and analytical devices—configured, loop-checked, and commissioned for harsh water and wastewater service.

Drives & starters

VFD and motor control coordinated with PLC/SCADA—speed, bypass, protection, and PID where the process demands it.

Industrial networks

EtherNet/IP, Modbus, secure remote access—from field devices through plant SCADA, aligned with IT and operations standards.

FAT / SAT

Witnessed factory testing and site commissioning—loops, interlocks, alarms, and tuning under real conditions.

Documentation

Native files, tag databases, narratives, and training—packaged for municipal closeout and warranty.
