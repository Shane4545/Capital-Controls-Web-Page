Where we focus

Municipal water infrastructure and specialty industrial controls

Most of our integration work is municipal water treatment, wastewater treatment, and pumping infrastructure. We also deliver controls for transit and selective industrial applications where the same documentation discipline and FAT/SAT rigor apply.

Primary Market: Municipal Water & Wastewater

Municipal water, wastewater, and pumping programs are the core of our practice. We supply, program, and commission PLC-based control systems with integrated HMI and SCADA for comprehensive process monitoring and control.

Water Treatment Plants

Typical systems we integrate: Raw water intake and low lift pumping, screening and grit removal, chemical dosing (coagulation, chlorination, pH adjustment), clarification systems including Actiflo and ballasted flocculation, gravity and membrane filtration, UV disinfection, high lift pumping and distribution, backwash systems, and clearwell level control.

Recent project example: Carleton Place WTP Expansion involved CompactLogix PLC programming with iFix SCADA workstation, process control narratives for each unit operation, and integration of Siemens instrumentation including magnetic flow meters and ultrasonic level transmitters.

We develop control logic for equipment monitoring and control, with PLC programs written using clear organized ladder logic and comprehensive comments to ensure programs can be understood by operators and programmers not involved in the original project.

Wastewater Treatment Plants

Typical systems we integrate: Influent pumping and screening, grit removal including vortex systems, primary clarification, biological treatment (activated sludge, MBR, SBR), aeration blowers and diffusers, secondary clarification, UV disinfection, sludge thickening and dewatering, digester control and mixing, and effluent pumping.

Recent project example: Carleton Place WWTP Expansion required phased commissioning to maintain continuous operation during the upgrade. We provided standalone PLC programming for the biological treatment process, integrated VFD control for aeration blowers, and developed HMI graphics displaying alarm data with necessary operator control interfaces.

Our approach supports upgrades that must coexist with running facilities through phased cutovers, temporary bypass logic, and documentation that operators and maintenance staff can follow under outage pressure.

Pumping Stations & Lift Stations

Configuration types: Sanitary sewage lift stations with submersible pumps and wet well level control, stormwater pumping stations with high-flow capacity, water booster stations with pressure control and variable speed drives, and raw water intake stations with traveling screens.

Standard features we implement: Duplex, triplex, or quad pump configurations with lead/lag/standby rotation, high and low level alarms with auto-dialer capability, wet well level control using ultrasonic transmitters, VFD speed control for flow matching, backup generator integration, and remote SCADA monitoring via cellular modem.

Recent project examples: McGee Street Lift Station (CCI-1413) included a SCADA panel with CompactLogix controller, PanelView touchscreen, and cellular modem to provide lift station control and monitoring with communication to the main plant. Golfview Subdivision Pumping Station (CCI-1413Q) featured similar architecture with duplex pump control and remote access capability.

Transit & Infrastructure

We have delivered control systems for transit applications including tunnel dewatering systems, station drainage pumping, and emergency backup systems. These projects typically involve coordination with transit authorities and integration with existing facility management systems.

Applications: TTC and LRT infrastructure projects requiring ruggedized control panels, redundant communication paths, and compliance with transit-specific electrical and safety standards.

Bridges & Conveyors

Bridge automation: We have supplied control systems for swing bridge automation, including motor control, position sensing, safety interlocks, and operator interfaces for manual and automatic operation modes.

Conveyor integration: Material handling and conveyor systems requiring discrete and motion control integrated with supervisory SCADA. These applications use the same Allen-Bradley CompactLogix platform and Ethernet/IP communication architecture as our municipal work.

Industrial Process Control

Applications we support: Chemical feed systems with precision dosing control, process water treatment for industrial facilities, cooling tower control with temperature and flow management, and compressed air systems with pressure regulation.

Industrial projects are scheduled around our municipal workload and represent a secondary market. However, they leverage the same engineering practices: documented ladder logic, testable operator interfaces, comprehensive commissioning, and complete as-built documentation.

Platform consistency across industries

Regardless of market sector, we standardize on Allen-Bradley CompactLogix and ControlLogix PLCs with FactoryTalk HMI, Siemens process instrumentation, and Ethernet/IP network architecture. This platform consistency ensures reliable parts availability, familiar programming environments for client maintenance staff, and proven integration patterns across all control system components.
