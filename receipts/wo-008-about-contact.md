## TECHNICAL NOTES

### Changes Align with Municipal Procurement Context
- Municipal RFPs typically require:
  - Quality management system registration (ISO 9001) ✓
  - Panel certification (CSA/UL) ✓
  - Manufacturer authorization letters (Siemens) ✓
  - Documented fabrication procedures ✓
  - As-built documentation discipline ✓

### Language Choices
- "Tender-ready" signals understanding of public procurement
- "Technical proposal" vs "inquiry" elevates formality
- "Procurement requirements" acknowledges compliance context
- "Factory inspection" and "photo registers" are shop-floor credibility markers
- "Owner audits" acknowledges municipal oversight expectations

### Avoided
- Generic "reach out" language
- "Contact us today" style CTAs
- Marketing superlatives ("best," "leading," "premier")
- Unsupported capability claims
- Template-style copy that could apply to any integrator
