Policy
------
Public pages should use CCI-owned or CCI-delivered evidence only: photos from your
jobs (shop or field), and SCADA/HMI captures from programs you integrated where
client policy allows. Do not commit stock aerial footage or generic stock video
as if it were company work.

Homepage hero VIDEO: assets/video/cci-hero-loop.mp4 — prefer AI image-to-video from your
still (scripts/build_hero_loop_ai.py + REPLICATE_API_TOKEN; see assets/video/SOURCE.txt),
or non-AI Ken Burns (scripts/build_hero_loop_from_still.py), or replace with edited footage.

Marketing refresh (PLC & pumping imagery)
-----------------------------------------
Several site photos pre-date current Rockwell catalog highlights (e.g. **CompactLogix 5380**
and **ControlLogix 5580** processors) and do not yet show **Flygt MultiSmart** hardware
on Xylem-led packages. When you re-run sync_job_photos.py, prioritize job folders that
document:
  • Field PLC enclosures where 5380/5580-class CPUs are clearly visible (with client approval).
  • Lift or booster stations where MultiSmart and your panel work appear in the same story.
  • Updated SCADA faceplates aligned to your present HMI standards.
