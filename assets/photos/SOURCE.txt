Capital Controls & Instrumentation — site photography sources
==============================================================

Policy
------
Public pages should use CCI-owned or CCI-delivered evidence only: photos from your
jobs (shop or field), and SCADA/HMI captures from programs you integrated where
client policy allows. Do not commit stock aerial footage or generic stock video
as if it were company work.

Synced from H:\Jobs (see scripts/sync_job_photos.py)
----------------------------------------------------
These JPEGs are resized from your job folders when you run the sync script:

  wtp-rockland-actiflo-panel.jpg   — OCWA East Rockland WTP (Actiflo panel photo)
  wtp-rockland-main-plc.jpg        — OCWA East Rockland WTP (main PLC)
  wtp-ingleside-field.jpg          — Ingleside WWTP Phase 1
  panel-csa-shop-primary.jpg       — Bishop Water PLC panel (CSA shop)
  panel-csa-shop-secondary.jpg     — Bishop Water PLC panel (second shop view)

To refresh or add “best current” shots: add (path, output filename) tuples to
JOBS[] in sync_job_photos.py, run:  python scripts/sync_job_photos.py

SCADA / HMI PNGs (exports from delivered systems)
-------------------------------------------------
Examples: scada-killaloe-overview.png, scada-pembroke-*.png, hmi-chalk-river-*.png
Replace these when you have newer, client-approved screen captures that represent
your current graphics standards.

Legacy / review individually before featuring
-------------------------------------------
Some older files in this folder may be phone snapshots or one-off exports.
If an image does not meet marketing quality, swap it in HTML for a synced job
photo or an updated SCADA export rather than using third-party stock.
