PLC Programming & Control System Integration

Supply, program, and commission Allen-Bradley PLC-based operational systems with integrated HMI for process monitoring and control. Our programming follows clear, documented ladder logic standards that ensure programs can be understood and maintained by operators and integrators who did not author the original delivery—critical for municipal infrastructure with multi-decade service lives.

  • Allen-Bradley CompactLogix and ControlLogix—1769 and 5069 CompactLogix (5370/5380 families) for pumping stations and small process systems; 1756 ControlLogix 5570/5580 processors (including L8x-class CPUs) for large WTP/WWTP applications
  • Xylem Flygt MultiSmart—Ethernet/Modbus integration with municipal PLCs for stations where MultiSmart ships on the Xylem package; joint checkout with Xylem when the contract is structured for shared startup
  • Custom ladder logic programming with clear documentation, descriptive tags, and memory block assignments
  • Process Control Narratives (PCN) describing control strategies, sequences, and interlocking logic
  • PID loop configuration and tuning for pumps, blowers, chemical dosing, and level control
  • Alarm and interlock programming with priority levels and operator-friendly messaging
  • Electronic deliverables in native format (RSLogix 5000/Studio 5000) and PDF for owner archives

CSA-Certified Control Panel Fabrication

Design and fabricate CSA-certified control panels engineered to meet site-specific requirements and environmental conditions. All panels are built to CSA/UL 508A standards in our certified shop with documented test procedures, photo registers, and comprehensive factory acceptance testing before shipment—delivering the quality documentation municipal procurement and ESA inspections require.

  • Panel types: PLC/SCADA control panels, VFD panels, soft starter panels, FVNR starter panels, MCC sections, network/RPU panels
  • NEMA 4/4X and IP65/IP66 ratings for outdoor and harsh environments—stainless steel (304/316) or painted carbon steel construction
  • Standard features: hinged doors with 3-point latching, interior lighting, laptop shelf with 120VAC/Ethernet outlets, panel heaters/thermostats for outdoor installations
  • Component integration: Allen-Bradley PLCs and I/O, Phoenix Contact terminal blocks and power supplies, surge protection on all I/O
  • Shop drawings and submittals: single-line electrical diagrams, wiring diagrams, terminal schedules, panel layout drawings
  • Factory Acceptance Testing (FAT): panel inspection, CSA certification verification, power supply testing, I/O card functionality, communication network testing, PLC program simulation

SCADA & HMI Development

Develop centralized SCADA for treatment plants and process facilities—real-time visualization, alarm management, and historical trending. HMI graphics follow P&ID conventions with navigation suited to shift operations.

  • SCADA platforms: GE iFix, FactoryTalk View SE, AVEVA Wonderware (InTouch/System Platform)
  • HMI hardware: Allen-Bradley PanelView Plus 7 touchscreens (7", 10", 12", 15" displays) with Ethernet/IP communication
  • Graphical displays: plant-wide overview screens, equipment faceplates, trend groups, alarm summaries
  • Alarm management: priority-based alarming, alarm annunciation, historical alarm logs, flood suppression
  • Operator control interfaces: Auto/Manual/Local mode selection, setpoint adjustment, equipment start/stop
  • Historical trending: process data logging, trend displays, report generation

Process Instrumentation Supply & Configuration

Supply and configure process instrumentation including flow, level, pressure, and analytical measurement devices with full commissioning support. We are authorized by Siemens for commissioning of Siemens Process Instrumentation in Ontario.

  • Magnetic flow meters: Siemens Sitrans FM (Mag 5000/6000), Endress+Hauser Promag—DN50 to DN600, 4-20mA output with HART
  • Ultrasonic level transmitters: Siemens Sitrans Probe LU, Endress+Hauser Prosonic—tank and wet well level monitoring
  • Pressure transmitters: Siemens Sitrans P (DS III, P300), Endress+Hauser Cerabar—gauge, differential, and hydrostatic pressure
  • Analytical instruments: Hach, YSI, Endress+Hauser—pH, turbidity, chlorine, dissolved oxygen
  • Float switches and level sensors: Magnetrol, Gems—high/low level alarms
  • Specifications: 4-20mA analog signals with HART protocol, stainless steel wetted materials (316L), IP68 submersible ratings, explosion-proof ratings where required
  • Yearly instrumentation verification: scheduled calibration and verification programs for municipal and industrial owners who need recurring evidence of instrument performance—the same type of ongoing field support described on the firm’s long-running public site.

VFD & Motor Starter Integration

Configure and commission variable frequency drives with seamless integration to PLC for pump and blower operation. Starter panels include drawings, submittals, CSA certification, configuration, and commissioning.

  • VFD applications: low lift pumps, high lift pumps, backwash pumps, waste activated sludge pumps, blowers (aeration, membrane scour), mixers
  • VFD brands: Allen-Bradley PowerFlex 525/755, ABB ACS580/ACS880, Danfoss VLT series
  • Starter types: VFD (variable frequency drive), soft starter (reduced voltage starting), FVNR (full voltage non-reversing), combination starters with motor protection
  • Integration features: Ethernet/IP or Modbus TCP communication to PLC, integrated bypass contactors, motor overload protection, current/power monitoring, remote start/stop and speed control from SCADA
  • Panel construction: NEMA 3R/4X or IP65 ratings, outdoor-rated enclosures, integrated disconnect and motor protection

Factory & Site Acceptance Testing

Factory acceptance testing, site startup, and operator training to ensure reliable operation at turnover. Commissioning follows systematic test procedures with documented results.

  • Factory Acceptance Testing (FAT): conducted at our shop before shipment—panel inspection and CSA label verification, power-up and voltage checks, PLC program simulation, HMI graphics navigation, communication network testing
  • Site Acceptance Testing (SAT): conducted at project site after installation—field device loop checks (4-20mA signals verified), motor rotation and VFD operation, interlock and alarm sequence testing, PID loop tuning under actual process loads, SCADA communication verification
  • Commissioning deliverables: commissioning reports with test results, as-built PLC programs and HMI graphics, instrument calibration certificates, loop check documentation
  • PLC output verification: forcing corresponding outputs from PLC program to verify field device operation
  • Process performance testing: support during startup to verify control sequences under actual operating conditions

Network Infrastructure & Remote Access

Integrate Ethernet/IP and fiber optic networks for reliable communication between field devices, PLCs, and SCADA workstations. Enable remote SCADA access via cellular modem for off-site monitoring, alarm notification, and troubleshooting support.

  • Managed switches: Allen-Bradley Stratix 5200/5800 series, Cisco Industrial Ethernet—VLAN, QoS, RSTP, DLR (Device Level Ring)
  • Fiber optic: single-mode fiber for long distances (>2km), multi-mode fiber for building-to-building (up to 2km), LC/SC connectors
  • Communication protocols: Ethernet/IP (primary for Allen-Bradley PLCs, VFDs, HMIs), Modbus TCP (third-party devices), Modbus RTU RS485 (legacy serial devices), HART (analog instruments)
  • Remote access: cellular modems (Sierra Wireless, Digi), VPN routers for secure remote access, static IP or dynamic DNS services
  • Network panels: Ethernet switches, fiber optic converters, cellular modems in NEMA-rated enclosures

Documentation & Training

Deliver complete as-built documentation including PLC programs, HMI graphics, wiring diagrams, and operation and maintenance manuals. Operator training sessions are structured around real plant tasks and equipment operation.

  • Shop drawings: panel general arrangement drawings, single-line electrical diagrams, wiring diagrams and terminal schedules, panel layout and component location drawings
  • Control system documentation: PLC program files (native RSLogix format + PDF), HMI graphics files (FactoryView/PanelView format + PDF), tag database with descriptions and I/O assignments, Process Control Narratives (PCN), network architecture diagrams
  • Instrumentation documentation: instrument data sheets and specifications, calibration certificates and range settings, installation and mounting details, wiring termination schedules
  • As-built records: marked-up drawings showing field changes, final PLC/HMI programs after commissioning, updated P&IDs with actual tag numbers, network configuration files
  • Operation & Maintenance manuals: equipment operation procedures, maintenance schedules, troubleshooting guides, spare parts lists, manufacturer literature
  • Operator training: on-site training (4-8 hours typical), PLC/HMI navigation and control mode selection, alarm acknowledgement and troubleshooting, setpoint adjustment procedures, emergency shutdown procedures
