Municipal water & wastewater themes

Our internal engineering library and project history include multi-PLC water treatment architectures—main plant PLCs, filter skids, low-lift and elevated storage, and centralized SCADA addressing—consistent with how older and modernized WTP/WWTP sites are structured. Upgrades often combine new Rockwell or SCADA layers with legacy telemetry (radio, leased line, or fiber) that must be phased out carefully.

SCADA / PLC modernization

Typical engagements replace obsolete HMI servers, consolidate tag databases, normalize alarm lists, and re-cut graphics to a plant standard—while keeping processes online through temporary graphics, shadow tags, or parallel operation windows.
